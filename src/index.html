WORK

COOKING FOR TWO

Can't decide what to cook for your date? Cooking For Two is a terminal app that allows you to impress a special someone in your life with delicious date night recipes for a variety of situations! Whether you want to make a meal, a dessert or simply a light snack, Cooking For Two has the recipe for you! Cooking For Two takes into account your date's dietary preferences, with options for vegans and people who are gluten intolerant.
